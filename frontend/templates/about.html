Our Story

Since 1987, Elegance Perfumery has perpetuated the ancestral art of French perfumery, creating exceptional fragrances that capture the essence of modern elegance.

The Art of Perfumery

Founded by Marie-Claire Dubois in the hills of Grasse, Elegance Perfumery was born from a passion for exceptional raw materials and craftsmanship passed down from generation to generation.

Each creation is the result of a meticulous artisanal process, where tradition and innovation meet to give birth to unique and timeless olfactory compositions.
